Lens 24-70mm f/2.8L II USM

The Lens 24-70mm f/2.8L II USM is a high-performance L standard zoom lens with a large aperture and a wide focal-length range. Magnification-type chromatic aberration at wider angles is corrected using three aspheric lens elements, a Super UD lens element, and two UD lens elements, resulting in superior image quality. With a new reduced length and fast yet silent autofocus, full-time mechanical manual focus, and a circular aperture for beautiful soft-focus images, this lens is made even easier to use. This lens is also ideal for close-ups, with a minimum focusing distance of 0.38m and a magnification of 0.29x..

Lens 16-35mm f/2.8L III USM

This large-diameter wide-angle zoom lens is ideal for wide-angle shots and delivers bright, high-quality images even in low-light situations. The L-series lenses are designed with two large-diameter double-surface aspheric GMo lenses, one ground aspheric lens, and two UD lenses to achieve high image quality from the center to the edge of the frame. Distortion and chromatic aberration are also significantly reduced, particularly at the wide end. Flaring and ghosting are also reduced by the Subwavelength Structure Coating (SWC) and Air Sphere Coating (ASC) coating. The dust-proof and drip-proof structure has been applied to the key areas of the lens to help photographers withstand challenging conditions.

- Max aperture: f/2.8
